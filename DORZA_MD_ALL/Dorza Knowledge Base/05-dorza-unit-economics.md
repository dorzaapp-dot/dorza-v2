> Last updated: March 2026 | Status: Pre-revenue estimates. Update with actuals once clients are onboarded.

|-----------|---------|--------|-----|

## Margins per tier

| Metric | Starter | Growth | Pro |
|--------|---------|--------|-----|
| Monthly revenue | $199 | $349 | $549 |
| Monthly cost | $18 | $41 | $165 |

Note: Pro tier margin is lower due to ad spend pass-through. Excluding ad spend, Pro margin is ~85%.

## Client acquisition cost (CAC)

Target CAC: $100–$200 via door-to-door canvassing + referral programs.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| CAC target | $100–$200 |
| CAC:LTV ratio (Growth) | 28–57x |
| Payback period (Growth) | < 1 month |

CAC is currently near-zero (founder-led sales, no paid marketing). Referral program ($100 credit per referral) will be the primary ongoing acquisition cost.

## Time investment per client

| Activity | Time | Frequency |
|----------|------|-----------|
| Initial setup (all 7 phases) | ~3.5 hours | Once |
| Ongoing maintenance | ~1 hour | Monthly |
| Content generation + scheduling | ~30 min | Weekly (automated after first batch) |

At 3.5 hours setup + 1 hr/month ongoing, a Growth client yields ~$308/month in gross profit for ~1 hour of ongoing work. Effective hourly rate: ~$308/hr.

## Assumptions and risks

- **AI API costs may decrease:** Model pricing has been trending down. Margins could improve over time.
- **AI API costs may increase:** Mitigated by diversifying across providers and caching common outputs.
- **Churn may be higher than 5%:** Local businesses close, change ownership, or cut costs. Offset by delivering visible, consistent results.
- **These are pre-revenue estimates.** Update cost-per-client with actual data after the first 5 clients. The AI API costs in particular may be higher or lower than estimated depending on actual usage patterns.
