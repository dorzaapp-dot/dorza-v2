## Pricing principles

- Always undercut traditional agencies by 60–70%
- Growth tier is the default recommendation for most clients
- Never discount below founding client pricing — it sets a floor
- Monthly retainer is the primary revenue driver; setup fees cover onboarding costs
- No lock-in contracts. Month-to-month. Confidence in retention through results.
