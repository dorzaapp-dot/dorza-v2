# Dorza — Company Overview

> Last updated: March 2026 | Status: Pre-launch

## What Dorza is

Dorza is an AI-native digital agency that provides local small businesses in Sydney with a complete done-for-you solution to get online and stay visible. We build websites, manage social media, set up Google Business profiles, deploy customer service chatbots, and run ongoing digital marketing.

**The model in one sentence:** We charge agency-level fees but use AI automation to deliver at near-zero marginal cost per client — giving us SaaS-like margins (85–91% gross) while clients get white-glove service.

## The core thesis

Traditional digital agencies charge $1,500–$5,000/month and require teams of designers, copywriters, and account managers. Most local businesses can't afford this, so they go without.

In 2025–2026, AI tools reached a tipping point where it became possible to generate production-quality websites, social media content, marketing copy, and customer service bots at near-zero marginal cost. Dorza exploits this arbitrage: we deliver agency-quality work at prices small businesses can actually afford, with 90%+ of the delivery automated.

**Clients get:** A professional website live in 24 hours, social media on autopilot, Google Business fully optimised, and an AI chatbot handling enquiries 24/7. They pay one predictable monthly fee and do nothing.

**We get:** Recurring revenue at 85–91% gross margins, with ~1 hour/month of ongoing time per client after initial setup.

## What Dorza is not

- Not a SaaS product. We don't give clients a tool to use. We do it for them.
- Not a traditional agency. We don't have large teams. AI does the heavy lifting.
- Not a website builder. Websites are one component of a full digital presence package.
- Not a marketplace or platform. We are the service provider, end to end.

## Competitive advantages

1. **Speed:** Website live in 24 hours vs 2–4 weeks from traditional agencies.
2. **Price:** 60–70% cheaper than comparable agency services.
3. **Scalability:** Adding clients doesn't require hiring. The AI OS handles incremental load.
4. **Retention:** Clients stay because they see consistent, visible results.
5. **Switching cost:** Once we manage website, social, Google, and chatbot, it's very sticky.
6. **Continuous improvement:** As AI models improve, our service quality improves automatically.
